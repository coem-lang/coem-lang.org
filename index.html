Natural and literary language

In the Pythonic way, code the way you write. Operators and keywords are designed for a wealth of poetic expression. 

to sleep—when—:
    when.

sleep—"tonight"—
sleep—"never"—

Regular expressions

Express multiplicity and place ideas into juxtaposition. Use regular expressions (a sequence of characters specifying a search pattern) in identifiers and values.

let me be true
let se(e|a) be "blue"
let mis(t|sed) be "thick"
let mou?rning be "dark"

Personalisation

Teach the compiler how to understand and treat you. Directives allow you to use different features and change the way you receive feedback.

#using palimpsest
#using daisychain
#be gentle
